# Spotify Portal -- Mock Demo Interview

> **External Candidate Handout**

---

## Overview

As part of your interview process, you will deliver a **20-minute demo** of a feature within Spotify Portal to a mixed audience.

**This exercise evaluates:**

- Your demo and storytelling skills
- Your ability to communicate across technical and non-technical audiences
- Your product intuition
- Your ability to synthesize customer feedback

---

## Interview Format

### Part 1 -- Live Demo (20 minutes)

You will demo **one** of the following:

- **Catalog**
- **Soundcheck**
- **Portal Studio**

You may choose whether to:

- Take questions **during** the demo
- Take questions **at the end**

Your audience will include:

| Role | Perspective |
|------|-------------|
| Sales stakeholder | Business value & customer impact |
| Engineer | Technical depth & feasibility |
| Leadership / Executive | Strategic alignment & vision |

### Part 2 -- Internal Recap (10 minutes)

After the demo, you will provide an internal recap **as if speaking to Product and Engineering teams**.

We will be looking for:

- What you learned from the "customer"
- Risks or gaps you identified
- Suggested next steps

### Part 3 -- Reflection & Product Feedback (10 minutes)

We'll ask you about:

- Your experience learning the product
- What worked well
- What could be improved
- Any product insights you formed

## Topic Options

You may choose to demo **one** of the following:

### 1. Catalog

The Catalog is the foundational **system of record** within Portal. It provides visibility into software components, services, ownership, dependencies, and metadata across the organization.

**It helps teams:**

- Discover services and ownership
- Improve transparency
- Reduce knowledge silos
- Standardize metadata and governance

> **Documentation:** [Catalog docs](https://backstage.spotify.com/docs/portal/features/catalog/)

---

### 2. Soundcheck

Soundcheck is Portal's **scorecard and standards engine**. It enables organizations to define and measure engineering standards across teams and services.

**It helps teams:**

- Define best practices
- Track compliance against standards
- Improve software quality
- Drive engineering consistency at scale

> **Documentation:** [Soundcheck docs](https://backstage.spotify.com/docs/portal/features/soundcheck/)

---

### 3. Portal Studio

Portal Studio enables teams to **build, customize, and extend** their Portal experience. It supports the creation of plugins, workflows, templates, and UI extensions.

**It helps teams:**

- Extend platform capabilities
- Automate workflows
- Customize internal developer experiences
- Integrate external systems

> **Documentation:** [Portal Studio docs](https://backstage.spotify.com/docs/portal/features/portal-studio/)

---

## What We're Looking For

> We are **not** evaluating memorization.

We are looking for your ability to:

- **Tell a clear story** -- Structure a narrative, not just a feature walkthrough
- **Communicate technical ideas effectively** -- Make complex concepts accessible
- **Translate value across different stakeholders** -- Tailor messaging to the audience
- **Think critically about product tradeoffs** -- Demonstrate product intuition
- **Provide thoughtful feedback** -- Share genuine observations and suggestions

---

## Preparation Guidance

We recommend:

- [ ] Exploring the demo environment thoroughly
- [ ] Thinking about the **problem** the feature solves, not just the feature itself
- [ ] Structuring your demo around **value**, not just functionality
- [ ] Being ready to handle questions from a mixed audience
- [ ] Preparing your internal recap framing ahead of time
